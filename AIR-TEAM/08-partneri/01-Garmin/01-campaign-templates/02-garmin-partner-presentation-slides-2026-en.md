# Garmin Partner Presentation 2026

## Slide 1: Our Partnership Vision for Garmin

- In 2026, our goal is to elevate `Garmin` within `AIR TEAM` from product presence to a strategically developed partner brand.
- We aim to deliver stronger visibility, higher presentation standards, and long-term brand support.
- A key priority for the year is completing the `AIR TEAM` rebrand and creating a more professional presentation of `Garmin` products and services.

## Slide 2: Our Main Focus This Year

- completing the `AIR TEAM` rebrand,
- enhancing the presentation quality of partner brands,
- strengthening alignment between marketing, sales, and service,
- delivering a better customer experience across both online and offline touchpoints.

## Slide 3: How We Want to Strengthen the Garmin Brand

- increasing `Garmin` visibility across key customer touchpoints,
- communicating the value of products and solutions more clearly,
- connecting product communication with service expertise,
- supporting the brand consistently rather than through one-off activities.

## Slide 4: Web and Digital Presentation

- higher-quality presentation of `Garmin` products, solutions, and services,
- a clearer website structure and improved customer navigation,
- stronger emphasis on authorization, expertise, and trust,
- a stronger role for the website as both a sales and information channel.

## Slide 5: Blogs and Educational Content

- regular articles on avionics, product updates, and practical applications,
- educational content that supports customer decision-making,
- case studies and installation references,
- long-term trust building through expertise.

## Slide 6: Ongoing Communication

- a year-round content calendar,
- regular social media communication,
- consistent inclusion of `Garmin` in newsletters,
- clear alignment between content, campaigns, and business priorities.

## Slide 7: The Kunovice Hangar as Part of the Partnership Presentation

- we also want the Kunovice hangar interior to reflect the `Garmin` partnership,
- the goal is to increase the brand's physical visibility during customer visits,
- the environment should reinforce trust, professionalism, and the quality of the partnership,
- the hangar should function as a presentation space, not just an operational facility.

## Slide 8: Which Offline Elements We Want to Use

- subtle interior elements with clear `AIR TEAM` + `Garmin` partnership branding,
- materials for meetings and visits that support a professional joint presentation,
- thoughtful integration of the brand and its story into the interior environment.

## Slide 9: How We Use Garmin Cash Back

- we reinvest it into visible brand support,
- we fund marketing and presentation materials,
- we support both online and offline execution,
- we use cash back as a tool for building long-term partnership value.

## Slide 10: Value for Garmin

- stronger brand visibility among relevant customers,
- a stronger connection between `Garmin`, expertise, and service,
- a higher-quality customer experience,
- more opportunities for inquiries, installations, and follow-up service,
- a more professional brand presentation aligned with the `AIR TEAM` direction for 2026.
