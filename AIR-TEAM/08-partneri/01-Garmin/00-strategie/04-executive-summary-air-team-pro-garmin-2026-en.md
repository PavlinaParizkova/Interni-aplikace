# Executive Summary: AIR TEAM for Garmin 2026

## Summary

In 2026, `AIR TEAM` wants to take its partnership with `Garmin` to a more visible, higher-quality, and more systematic level. Our goal is not only to sell and service `Garmin` products, but to strengthen the brand over the long term across both online and offline environments.

## Our Direction for 2026

The main framework for this year is the completion of the `AIR TEAM` rebranding and a more professional presentation of partner brands. In the case of `Garmin`, this means a stronger emphasis on better brand visibility, higher presentation quality, connecting product communication with service expertise, and consistent marketing support throughout the year.

## Key Areas Where We Want to Invest Our Energy

### Web and Digital Presentation

We want to improve the presentation of `Garmin` products, solutions, and services on the website so that customers can understand the benefits of our offering more quickly, navigate it more easily, and perceive `AIR TEAM` as a trustworthy expert partner.

### Content and Regular Communication

We want to support the `Garmin` brand through blogs, educational articles, case studies, regular posts, and newsletters. The goal is to increase `Garmin` brand awareness among customers and build trust through content that is useful and professionally relevant.

### Offline Presentation in Kunovice

The partnership vision also includes the brand's physical presentation in the hangar in Kunovice. We want to incorporate `Garmin` into the interior decor and presentation elements so that customers perceive the brand during in-person interactions as well. The hangar should function as a space that confirms the quality of the partnership and the professional standard of the presentation.

### Use of Garmin Cash Back

We want to use the cash back as an active brand support tool. We plan to reinvest these funds into marketing materials, promotional items, offline presentation in the hangar, and other visually high-quality outputs that will help increase the long-term visibility of the `Garmin` brand.

## What Garmin Gains from This

- greater brand visibility among relevant customers,
- a stronger connection between `Garmin` and expertise, service, and trust,
- higher-quality brand presentation aligned with the new direction of `AIR TEAM`,
- more opportunities for business, installations, and follow-up service,
- more consistent and systematic long-term brand support within our environment.

## One-Sentence Expression of Our Vision

`AIR TEAM` wants to be a partner for `Garmin` that not only represents the brand, but also purposefully elevates it in 2026 to a higher level of visibility, trust, and presentation quality.
